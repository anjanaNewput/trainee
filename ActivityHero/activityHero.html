MarketingHero FAQs

I already have a registration system. Can't you just send me traffic?

Most families sign up for 2-5 camps or activities per year, per child. Our goal is to make that experience as easy as possible -- which means letting parents register for all their camps right here on ActivityHero. We know it's a little less convenient for providers, but we're committed to making the best possible experience for parents.

What if I don't have an online registration system?

If you don't have an online registration system, you should talk to us about SuperHero. Our SuperHero system was designed from the ground up to be the ideal registration system for camps & activities. (It's really reasonably priced, too.) Learn more about SuperHero now >

How will I get paid?

ActivityHero collects payment directly from parents. Every two weeks, we'll send you a check or direct deposit all payments, minus our fees.

What information will come with the registration?

Most camps and activities collect standard information like names, birthdates, emergency contact numbers,and allergy information . In addition, you can set up custom data fields for any other questions you'd like families to answer.

How will you send me the registrations?

You'll be alerted by email or text the moment a registration is completed. Or, if you prefer, we can set up a data integration to your registration system.

How will you know when my camp or activity is full?

We recommend that you offer at least 5 seats in each activity or camp for ActivityHero families. When those fill up, you can open additional seats. In this way, you can ensure that you're never oversold.
